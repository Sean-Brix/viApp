# ViApp Installation Guide

## For Students and Administrators

### System Requirements
- Android 5.0 (Lollipop) or higher
- Internet connection
- Bluetooth capability (for device pairing)

## Installation Steps

### 1. Download the APK

Get the ViApp APK file from:
- School IT department
- Provided download link
- Shared storage (Google Drive, etc.)

### 2. Enable Unknown Sources

Before installing, you need to allow installation from unknown sources:

1. Open **Settings** on your Android device
2. Go to **Security** (or **Privacy**)
3. Find **Unknown Sources** or **Install Unknown Apps**
4. Enable installation from your file manager or browser

**Note:** The exact steps may vary depending on your Android version.

### 3. Install the APK

1. Locate the downloaded `viapp.apk` file (usually in Downloads folder)
2. Tap on the APK file
3. Review the permissions
4. Tap **Install**
5. Wait for installation to complete
6. Tap **Open** or find the app in your app drawer

### 4. First Launch

When you first open ViApp:

1. You'll see the login screen
2. **Students:** Use the credentials provided by your school
3. **Administrators:** Use your admin credentials

### 5. Grant Permissions

The app will request permissions for:
- **Location** (required for Bluetooth device discovery)
- **Bluetooth** (required for connecting to health monitoring devices)
- **Internet** (required for syncing data)

Please allow all permissions for the app to work properly.

## Using the App

### For Students

1. **Login** with your credentials
2. **Connect Device** (if you have a wearable health monitor)
   - Go to Settings
   - Select "Connect Device"
   - Follow the pairing instructions
3. **View Dashboard** to see your latest vitals
4. **Check Alerts** for any health notifications
5. **View History** to see your health trends
6. **Update Profile** in Settings if needed

### For Administrators

1. **Login** with admin credentials
2. **Dashboard** shows overview of all students
3. **Monitor Students** in real-time
4. **View Alerts** for students requiring attention
5. **Manage Students** - add, edit, or deactivate
6. **Register Devices** for students
7. **View Statistics** for school-wide health data

## Troubleshooting

### App Won't Install
- Ensure "Unknown Sources" is enabled
- Check if you have enough storage space
- Try restarting your device
- Make sure you downloaded the complete APK file

### Can't Login
- Check your internet connection
- Verify your username and password
- Contact your administrator for credential issues
- Ensure the school server is online

### Device Won't Connect (Bluetooth)
- Ensure Bluetooth is enabled on your phone
- Location permission must be granted
- Keep the device close to your phone during pairing
- Make sure the device is charged and turned on
- Try forgetting the device and pairing again

### Data Not Syncing
- Check your internet connection
- Ensure you're logged in
- Try refreshing by pulling down on the screen
- Logout and login again
- Contact your administrator if the issue persists

### App Crashes or Freezes
- Close and reopen the app
- Clear the app cache (Settings > Apps > ViApp > Storage > Clear Cache)
- Restart your device
- Uninstall and reinstall the app (your data is stored on the server)

## Privacy & Security

- Your health data is encrypted and stored securely
- Only authorized school staff can access your information
- Your login credentials should be kept private
- Report any suspicious activity to your administrator

## Support

For technical support, contact:
- **School IT Department**
- **School Health Office**

For urgent health concerns, contact:
- **School Nurse/Clinic**
- **Emergency Services (911)**

## Updates

When a new version is available:
1. Download the new APK
2. Install it (it will update the existing app)
3. Your data will be preserved

## Uninstallation

To remove the app:
1. Go to Settings > Apps
2. Find "ViApp"
3. Tap "Uninstall"

**Note:** Your data will remain on the school server even after uninstalling.
